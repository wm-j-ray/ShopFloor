# Timeline Entry

> **Purpose:** Records a single dated fact, event, or condition in the master chronology of a work of fiction — the atomic unit of story time.
>
> **Instance naming:** When creating an instance, save as `[ID]_Name.md`, set `profile_version` and `date_instantiated`.
